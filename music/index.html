About

Andrew Holloway is a genre-fluid composer, producer, and multi-instrumentalist based in Perth, Australia. Starting on piano at the age of 3 and beginning formal lessons at 8, Andrew's music journey has spanned orchestral compositions, rock, pop, and more recently, electronic-infused styles. His sound resists neat categorisationâ€”anchored by melody, shaped by emotion, and driven by an exploratory spirit.

Influenced more by moods than by specific artists, Andrew draws inspiration from everything from Jacob Collier's harmonic complexity to the soaring synths of FM-84 and Porter Robinson. His recent works include collaborations such as his 2025 single with FoofyFox, and past performances like his appearance at his hometown's local MADD concert.

Whether it's an ambient instrumental or a vibrant synth track, Andrew aims to craft music that feels cinematic, honest, and just a little unpredictable.
